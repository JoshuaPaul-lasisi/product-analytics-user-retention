## Product Overview
This analysis represents an online retail platform where users purchase physical products through discrete transactions.

## Users
The users are the registered customers, each identified by a unique customer ID. Only users with a valid Customer ID are included in the analysis.

## Core Value Action
The core value action is the purchase of a product. It is represented by an invoice. One purchase might involve more than one item, but it is treated as a single purchase. Therefore, that will be considered as a single successful user action.

## Activation
Activation point is the completion of the user's first successful purchase on the platform.

## Retention
This is completing other purchases over a certain period of time after their first purchase.

## Business Questions
This analysis will anser these (more question could come up):
- how many users return after their first purchase?
- how quickly do they repeat their actions
- where is the largest drop-off in repeat usage.
